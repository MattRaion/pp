Flyin Fat
Flyin Fat's mission is to fight overweight in the funny sense of the phrase.
We want to provide players with competitive, fun, and dynamic gameplay in our game.
Flyin Fat is an online arcade game. Players take on the role of the fat characters who have problems with keeping their balance. Up to 10 players can take part in the game, competing in such disciplines as:

Runninfat - a race of fatters, the run is based on the fact that the player's main task is to maintain balance so that his character does not fall down while avoiding obstacles.

Fatnament - a tournament where the characters slap each other in the face, and the one whose character can last longer wins.

Fattle - food battle, players are teleported to the board where they must find weapons, ammunition and finish off the rest of the opponents, the last one alive wins. The game board is set in the center of a large donut that shrinks over time, limiting the battlefield.

The player with the most points wins. There is also a general ranking with a summary of all matches.
