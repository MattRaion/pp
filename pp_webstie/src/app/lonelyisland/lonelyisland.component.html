Lonely Island
Lonely Island is a 2D survival game with story elements.
The player takes on Matt's role, who, together with his second half, are coming back by plane from a vacation in Europe. However, during the flight, something goes wrong. Lousy weather and a pilot mistake are factors that force the plane to crash in the middle of the ocean. After the catastrophe, Matt wakes up on the beach of a seemingly desert island.

He begins his struggle to survive. He must explore the deserted island to find other survivors from the crashed plane, hoping that his love is also among them. But, the island hides a dark secret.
