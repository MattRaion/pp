Datenshi: Reborn
DATENSHI's mission is to engage the cyber shinobi world with a fantastic story and good arcade fun.
DATENSHI is a two-dimensional action platform game with RPG elements. The player personages in the role of a Japanese Shinobi named Shoku, who pursues his best friend's kidnapper. It turns out that it is an organized activity during the pursuit, and the kidnapper himself is more than just a usual bandit. Shoku has to face various traps and mercenaries who have the mission to stop him.
